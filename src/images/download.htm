Specialist Textile Manufacturing

All of our fabrics are expertly engineered to be used in a variety of applications across a diverse range of markets from acute medical environments to retail mattress covers.

We have experience as a traditional technical textile converter and have a specialist understanding of flame retardant treatments to fabrics using inherent yarn, back coating or the impregnation of chemicals during the finishing process. We deal with a range of sectors including contract bedding, upholstery, curtaining and a wide range of industrial applications.

We also have an extensive range of coated fabrics which have been specifically developed to meet and exceed the demands of the medical and healthcare markets. By combining our knowledge of fabrics with different coating techniques we have developed a comprehensive range of materials; all of which can be used to produce an extensive range of healthcare products within the pressure relief, incontinence and patient moving & handling sectors.

Redwood are highly respected within the trade and have been invited to work with Industry Experts such as the BHTA (British Healthcare Trades Association) on a number of occasions. We are at the forefront of driving change in the technical textiles industry to deliver real benefits for the end user, as demonstrated by the properties of our Xtreme fabric coating. We have a unique understanding of developing technical textiles and the manufacturing processes of how they can weld together, to provide a product that is truly fit for purpose and provides real differentiation to the end product.

We pride ourselves on our commitment to quality and rapid turnaround, but above all our ability to produce cost-effective product runs and prototypes. Many manufacturers can supply orders in the thousands but don't show the same interest in supplying ten. Our energy and drive to supply the best applies equally to a single prototype or a worldwide shipment of thousands per week. We can support new manufacture and improvement programs with unrivalled efficiency.

We ensure all our fabric and textile ranges are designed to meet their required purpose and are committed to providing quality products.
